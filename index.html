About AGARWOOD

AGARWOOD is an innovative Web3 NFT mining game built on the WAX blockchain, where players harvest AGAR tokens by owning and staking unique Agarwood NFTs. The game features a structured economy with sustainable tokenomics, crafting mechanics, and an engaging play-to-earn model.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 300,000,000 AGAR tokens

Mining Rewards: Distributed through NFT staking.

Crafting & Upgrading: Burn mechanism to balance the economy.

Marketplace & Transactions: AGAR used for NFT trading and upgrades.
