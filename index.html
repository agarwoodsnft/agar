About AGARWOOD

AGARWOOD is an innovative Web3 NFT mining game built on the WAX blockchain, where players harvest AGAR tokens by owning and staking unique Agarwood NFTs.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 300,000,000 AGAR tokens

Token Contract: token.agar

Mining Rewards: Distributed through NFT staking.

Crafting & Upgrading: Burn mechanism to balance the economy.

Marketplace & Transactions: AGAR used for NFT trading and upgrades.

Liquidity Plan

To ensure a stable token economy, we will implement the following liquidity strategy:

  • Initial liquidity pool funding with 50,000 WAX.
  • Gradual buyback and burns to prevent inflation.
  • 10% of all in-game transactions allocated to liquidity reinforcement.
  • Partnerships with DeFi platforms for additional liquidity incentives.
